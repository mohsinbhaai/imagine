SERVICES FOR ARCHITECTS & DESIGNERS

For over a decade, we at Imagine have worked with and for some of the finest Architects, Interior, Landscape and Lighting Designers in the world. We can take over the Design and Specification of various Electronic Low Voltage systems while integrating into the desired aesthetics of the spaces.

With a dedicated Design team, we become an extension of the Architects and Designers teams on projects. At Imagine, we have a full-time CAD department, with a qualified Architect onboard, with an understanding of Interior Design, MEP Services, and Lighting Layouts. This enables us to understand multiple trades, and deliver on the overall aesthetic vision while integrating technology into the spaces.

“The best technologies are an extension of architecture space and furnishings. Architects, designers, and builders choose Imagine first because of our experience and approach to making technology blend seamlessly into spaces.”
