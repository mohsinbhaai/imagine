WHO WE ARE

OVER A DECADE OF EXCELLENCE

“We do not want to be know as the biggest integration company, we want to be known as the BEST Integration company in the region and in the world.”

Imagine Group Limited and Imagine Technologies LLC were founded in 2004 and 2005 respectively, and are products of the passion, imagination and innovation of the Founders, who took their personal love of music and audio-visual technology to a space where they could share their expertise and vision with like-minded others and to fulfill a knowledge gap in the marketplace, driven by the need to raise the bar in terms of what was possible when it came to music systems, home theater and home automation in the Middle East, Africa and the Indian Sub-continent.

The business has evolved and grown over the years, with an ever-expanding portfolio of world-class brands and products, systems and technologies as well as a similarly passionate team of specialists and technicians, some of whom have been with us since inception.

Our success over the last decade has always relied on two guiding principles: working meticulously towards creating amazing experiences for our clients; and building relationships that evolve way beyond project completion.

Imagine is an equal opportunity employer, and integrity and business ethics are valued.

OUR MISSION

“To deliver bespoke, information technology, high-performance entertainment, automation and related solutions, by combining cutting-edge technologies with the finest products and consultative services in the world for our clients.”

WHY IMAGINE?

The following details are why so many of the most discriminating clients have chosen to work with Imagine in the past, and continue to call on us again and again.

The best technologies are simple to use. Decades of experience have taught us what clients love and what they have no interest in. The user interface for our systems has earned many awards—even from our industry peers.

SENSITIVE TO INTERIOR DESIGN

The best technologies meld into the home’s architecture and furnishings. Architects, designers, and builders choose Imagine first because of our experience and approach to making technology blend seamlessly into spaces.

It’s not what we do, it’s how we do it. Imagine’s work is meticulous and we employ people that are passionate about quality. Our technicians consistently win peer-awards for their craftsmanship.

PARTNERSHIPS WITH INDUSTRY LEADERS

Everyone who works on a project is part of the Imagine family — carefully chosen employees and partners. Each Partner is a leader in their own right in their area of expertise.

FOCUSED ON YOUR EXPERIENCE, NOT GIZMOS
